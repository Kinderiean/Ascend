# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-05-02**

Ascend ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the Ascend mobile app collects, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have over it.

## 1. Data We Collect

When you use Ascend, we may collect the following data:

| Category | Examples | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Account data | Email address, hashed password (or OAuth identifier from Apple/Google) | Authentication, account recovery |
| Profile data | Display name, age, height, weight, units, dietary preference, skin type, workout location, sleep schedule | Personalize routines |
| Usage data | Goal text, Q&A answers from onboarding, custom tasks, completed tasks, streak count, XP | Drive in-app personalization and progress tracking |
| Subscription data | In-App Purchase transaction ID, subscription status, entitlement | Provide paid features |
| Device data | Operating system, app version, anonymized device identifier | Crash reporting, debugging |
| Crash data | Error stack traces (with PII scrubbed) | Diagnose and fix bugs |

**We do not collect:** real names, addresses, phone numbers, financial details, location, contacts, photos (unless you upload a goal photo, which is stored locally on your device only), microphone, camera, or precise health metrics from device sensors without your explicit permission.

## 2. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process data on the following bases:
- **Consent** — for analytics, crash reports, and any optional feature
- **Contract** — to deliver the subscription you purchase
- **Legitimate interests** — basic security, fraud prevention

You can withdraw consent at any time by signing out, deleting your account, or contacting us.

## 3. Third-Party Processors

We use the following processors:
- **Supabase Inc.** (hosted in EU) — authentication and account database
- **RevenueCat Inc.** (USA) — subscription receipt validation
- **Apple App Store / Google Play** — payment processing for subscriptions
- **Sentry GmbH** (hosted in EU) — anonymized crash and error reports
- **Resend** (USA) — transactional email (account confirmation, password reset)

Each processor has a Data Processing Agreement and processes data only on our instructions.

## 4. Data Retention

- Active accounts: data is kept while your account is active
- Deleted accounts: profile is soft-deleted immediately (hidden from queries) and hard-deleted after 30 days. Anonymized crash logs may be retained for 90 days for debugging
- Subscription records: retained for 7 years for tax / accounting compliance

## 5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws, you have the right to:
- **Access** — request a copy of your data
- **Correct** — fix inaccuracies
- **Delete** — erase your account (Settings → Delete my account; or email us)
- **Port** — receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON)
- **Object / Restrict** — opt out of analytics or specific processing

Email **ashutoshjr@gmail.com** to exercise any of these rights. We respond within 30 days.

## 6. Children

Ascend is not directed at children under 13. If you are under the age of consent in your country, do not use the app. If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it.

## 7. International Transfers

Data may be transferred to the USA (RevenueCat, Resend) under Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

## 8. Security

- All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.3
- Account tokens are stored on your device using OS-level secure storage
- Passwords are checked against the Have-I-Been-Pwned database on signup to prevent reuse of known-breached passwords
- Database access is gated by Row Level Security policies — users can only ever read or write their own data

## 9. AI / Health Information Disclaimer

Ascend's content (workouts, skincare, nutrition, sleep tips) is for informational purposes only and is not medical advice. Always consult a qualified professional for medical, dermatological, or psychological concerns. Citations to peer-reviewed research are provided where available; the presence of a citation does not constitute a medical recommendation.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via email or an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision.
